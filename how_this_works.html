The Scoring Algorithm

How 187 articles become 5 stories

Every article gets a numeric intelligence score. The top 5 go to Claude. Here's the exact math.

final_score = ( topic_score × source_multiplier × category_weight + entity_bonus + keyword_bonus ) × recency × novelty
source_multiplier = trust value from feeds.yaml (0.0 – 1.0)
entity_bonus = +3 priority-1 · +1.5 priority-2 (entity_priorities.json)
keyword_bonus = +2 tier-1 · +1 tier-2 · −2 noise words
recency = 0.85 if >18h old · 1.0 normal · 1.1 if <2h old
novelty = 0.3 – 1.0 · penalises similarity to recent analyzed titles
category_weight = per feed group in feeds.yaml
