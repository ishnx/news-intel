# /intelligence/weekly — Weekly Intelligence Digest

This folder's `index.html` shows the past 7 days of intelligence reports.
It is regenerated automatically by `build_indexes.py` after each pipeline run.

## Access

- **Live:** `https://[username].github.io/[repo]/intelligence/weekly/`
- **Local:** Open `index.html` in your browser

## Contents

Shows all reports from the last 7 days, ordered by date.
Provides a weekly perspective on intelligence themes.
